About the conference

The Revealing Connections: Leveraging Linked Open Data to discover knowledge through ResearchSpace is being organised as a part of the European Research Council funded project entitled Write - New Forms of Calligraphy in China: A Contemporary Cultural Mirror and a Framework for Attracting and Strengthening Research Excellence funded project entitled WenDAng - WRITE Digital Archive, together with the /DH.arc - Digital Humanities Research Center of the University of Bologna.
